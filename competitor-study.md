# Competitor Study

## Toptal
- First CTA: Hire Top Talent
- Trust: Strong vetting & testimonials
- Steal: Quality assurance
- Avoid: Slow onboarding

## Upwork
- First CTA: Post a Job
- Trust: Ratings & reviews
- Steal: Comparison filters
- Avoid: Low trust for hardware work

## Lemon.io
- First CTA: Get Matched
- Trust: Pre-vetted experts
- Steal: Speed
- Avoid: No team-based execution

Key Insight:
None support city-based execution clusters.
