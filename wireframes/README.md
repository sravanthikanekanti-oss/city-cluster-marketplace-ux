## Project Overview
This project explores a city-based cluster marketplace that helps companies quickly build execution-ready electronics teams within a selected city.

The UX focuses on trust, clarity, and fast decision-making for enterprise and startup users.

---

## Problem Statement
Companies often struggle to find reliable, verified electronics teams in specific cities.
The process is fragmented, time-consuming, and lacks transparency.

---

## Design Goals
- Reduce decision time
- Build trust through verification signals
- Provide clear cost and timeline expectations
- Keep flows simple and scannable

---

## Screen Flow Overview

### Screen 1: City Selection
Users select their preferred city to view locally available execution-ready teams.

**Key UX Decisions:**
- Limited city options to reduce cognitive load
- Clear CTA to move forward

---

### Screen 2: Engagement Recommendation
Recommended engagement models are shown based on the selected city.

**Key UX Decisions:**
- Side-by-side comparison
- Simple descriptions instead of technical jargon

---

### Screen 3: City Clusters & Trust Signals
Shows city-specific clusters with skills, verification badges, and delivery guarantees.

**Key UX Decisions:**
- Trust badges (verified, completed projects)
- Transparent timeline and cost ranges

---

### Screen 4: Next Steps & Commitment
User selects how they want to proceed (consultation, shortlisting, or direct engagement).

**Key UX Decisions:**
- Clear next actions
- No forced commitment

---

### Screen 5: Confirmation & Transparency
Final confirmation screen summarizing user selections and expectations.

**Key UX Decisions:**
- Summary-based reassurance
- Transparency before engagement
